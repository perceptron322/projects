About Company

Established in 1974 by entrepreneur Robert Thompson, Threads & Strides emerged as a fashion-forward brand committed to redefining the way people dressed. Starting as a small workshop, the company gained recognition for its craftsmanship, initially focusing on high-quality trousers and later diversifying into stylish jackets. As the '80s unfolded, Threads & Strides continued to evolve, incorporating new fabrics and designs into its repertoire. The '90s saw further diversification with the introduction of a line of comfortable yet chic shirts and a range of footwear that combined fashion with functionality. Expanding globally in the 2000s, Threads & Strides opened flagship stores in fashion capitals worldwide, solidifying its position as a global fashion powerhouse. The product range expanded to include a diverse array of clothing, from casual wear to formal attire. In response to growing environmental awareness in the 2010s, Threads & Strides embraced sustainability, implementing eco-friendly practices in production. The company became a symbol of responsible fashion, contributing to various social and environmental causes. Today, with nearly five decades of experience, Threads & Strides continues to evolve. The product line includes cutting-edge designs in shirts, jackets, shoes, and trousers, all crafted with a perfect blend of tradition and modernity. Committed to quality, style, and sustainability, Threads & Strides offers customers a wardrobe that reflects individuality while contributing to a better world. Looking towards the future, Threads & Strides remains dedicated to its legacy of innovation and timeless style, ensuring that the journey that began in 1974 continues to shape the fashion landscape for generations to come.

Company History

Innovation and Sustainability (2011-2015):

As the fashion landscape evolved, so did LuxeThreads. In 2011, the brand embraced innovation and sustainability, incorporating eco-friendly practices into its production processes. LuxeThreads became a pioneer in the use of ethically sourced fabrics and environmentally conscious manufacturing techniques, setting a new standard for responsible luxury fashion.

Global Expansion (2016-2020):

With a growing reputation for impeccable design and a commitment to sustainability, LuxeThreads expanded its presence globally. The brand opened flagship stores in fashion capitals such as Paris, Milan, and New York, becoming synonymous with sophisticated style on an international scale. Celebrities and influencers alike began to embrace LuxeThreads as a symbol of both fashion and ethical consciousness.

Adapting to Change (2021-Present):

In the face of a rapidly changing world, LuxeThreads has continued to adapt and innovate. The brand launched an e-commerce platform, providing customers with seamless access to their favorite pieces from the comfort of their homes. Collaborations with renowned designers and artists further solidified LuxeThreads as a trailblazer in the intersection of art and fashion.

The Future of Elegance:

As LuxeThreads looks towards the future, the brand remains committed to its core values of elegance, innovation, and sustainability. With each new collection, LuxeThreads seeks to inspire confidence and empower individuals to express their unique style. The journey that began in a small studio in Vogueville has transformed into a global phenomenon, and LuxeThreads continues to shape the future of upscale fashion. Join us on this journey as we weave the threads of luxury, sustainability, and timeless elegance into the fabric of tomorrow. LuxeThreads - Where Style Meets Substance.

Premium materials

When it comes to choosing materials for our shirts, natural fibers offer several benefits. Here are some reasons why using natural materials in our shirts is a good choice: Breathability: Natural fibers like linen or cotton allow our skin to breathe, keeping us cool in hot weather and warm in cold weather. This breathability helps regulate body temperature and prevents discomfort caused by sweating. Comfort: Clothing made from natural fibers is softer and more comfortable to wear compared to synthetic materials. Natural fibers feel gentle against the skin and provide a comfortable and cozy feeling throughout the day. Hypoallergenic: Natural fabrics such as silk, cotton, and linen are hypoallergenic in nature. They are less likely to cause skin irritations or allergies, making them suitable for people with sensitive skin. Sustainability: Natural materials are often more sustainable and eco-friendly compared to synthetic fabrics. They are biodegradable and have a lower environmental impact during production and disposal. By choosing shirts made from natural materials, you can enjoy the benefits of breathability, comfort, sustainability, and durability while supporting a more eco-friendly and skin-friendly wardrobe.
